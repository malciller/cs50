What's This Tool About?

So, I taught myself programming with a lot of help from LLMs. ChatGPT was a game-changer for me, helping me level up from struggling to learn, to landing a gig as a programmer. I'm a big fan of OpenAI (obviously), and GPT-4 blew my mind. But yeah, the usage limits are a bummer. I'm doing this course, right? And for my final project, I thought why not build something where folks like me can use GPT-4 with their own API keys. So, here we are!

How To:

Go back and click on "Register":

1. Create and account with a Username, Password, and Email Address. This info won't be shared ever.

2. You'll be prompted to enter your OpenAI API Key. Currently only works if you have access to the GPT-4 endpoints.

3. Login, and go to the "Chat" window to begin chatting with GPT-4-Turbo

4. Under the "Account" tab, you can update your email on file, password, or api key. You can also permanently delete your account and associated account information.

Enjoy

What's Up With Your Data?

Here's the deal with data:

  • Your chats, like what you send and get back, I'm gonna store and use them for data science stuff later.
  • Any personal info you share (like username, email, API keys) stays on the server for as long as you use this platform. You can wipe your account, including all that info, anytime from the Account page. But heads up, this won't delete your chat history.
Just by using the site, you're cool with all this. And hey, if you've got questions or concerns about your data, feel free to reach out to malciller@gmail.com.
